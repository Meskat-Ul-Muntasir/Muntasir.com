If you have created your own website, you will want to provide an email link. You can just give your e-mail ID without the link, but after copying and pasting your e-mail ID to the visitors, it will be a hassle to send the e-mail. Therefore, it is better to create a link which, opens the visitor's mail box when it is clicked. The code is given below.
CODE:
<a href="mailto:abc@x.com">Contact via email</a>
